📈 What is Investing & How Markets Work

Investing is how you grow your money over time by putting it into assets that increase in value.

Instead of letting money sit idle, you make it work for you.

🚫 Saving = storing money
✔ Investing = growing money

💰 What is Investing?

Investing means buying assets like stocks, bonds, or real estate with the goal of making a return.

In simple terms: You give your money to businesses or assets → they grow → your money grows.

  • ✔ Stocks → ownership in companies
  • ✔ Bonds → lending money
  • ✔ Real estate → property investment
💡 Investing is long-term thinking, not quick profit

🎯 Why People Invest

  • Build wealth over time
  • Beat inflation
  • Achieve financial freedom

If you don’t invest, inflation slowly reduces your money’s value.

⚠️ Money sitting idle = losing value over time

🏦 What is the Market?

The stock market is a place where people buy and sell ownership in companies.

Prices move based on:

  • Supply (sellers)
  • Demand (buyers)

More buyers → price goes up
More sellers → price goes down

💡 Market = auction of stocks happening every second

⚙️ How the Market Works

When you buy a stock, you’re buying it from someone else selling it.

The exchange matches buyers and sellers instantly.

  • You place a buy order
  • Someone sells
  • Trade happens → price is set
🎯 No magic—just buyers and sellers agreeing on a price

📊 Why Prices Move

  • Company performance
  • News & events
  • Investor emotions (fear & greed)

Short-term = emotional moves
Long-term = business performance

💡 In the short run, market is emotional. In the long run, it follows reality.

⚔️ Investor vs Trader

  • Investor: Long-term, focuses on companies
  • Trader: Short-term, focuses on price moves
🎯 Investing = patience
Trading = speed

🧠 Investor Mindset

Good investing is not about getting rich quickly.

It’s about making smart decisions consistently over time.

💡 Time in the market beats timing the market
